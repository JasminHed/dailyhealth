The Yoga Pose

The yoga positions you’ll be guided through are voiced with the help of AI, based on scripts carefully written by the creator of this space. Each pose is selected to support the state of mind you choose — offering a moment of grounding, stillness, or gentle energy. These sessions are meant to be a simple tool to reconnect with your body and breath in the flow of everyday life.
